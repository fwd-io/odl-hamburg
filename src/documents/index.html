Open Device Labs

The range of Smartphones and Tablets increases every day, and often it is not easy for web developers to test on all relevant devices. Open Device Labs provide a solution to this problem: Donated and rented devices are collected and made available, for free and for everyone.

ODL Hamburg

The Open Device Lab Hamburg is provided by the software agency LAUNCH/CO and is located in Hamburgs exciting neighbourhood St. Pauli, 5 minutes walking distance form the S-Bahn station "Feldstraße". Have a look at our devices and feel free to contact us if you have questions or feedback!
